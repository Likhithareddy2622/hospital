FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions regarding clinical appointments, medical insurance coverage, and inpatient visitor rules.

How can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

You can easily reschedule or cancel an appointment by calling our booking helpline at +1 (800) 555-0199 or emailing us at appointments@auracarehospital.com at least 24 hours prior to your scheduled time.

What insurance plans does AuraCare accept?

AuraCare Hospital works with almost all major health insurance providers. For a complete detailed list of approved insurance networks, check our Billing desk or email us with your policy provider name.

Are visitors allowed in the ICU wards?

To protect critically ill patients, visiting hours in ICU wards are restricted. Only immediate family members (maximum 2 at a time) are allowed to visit during designated hours (11:00 AM - 1:00 PM and 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM) after receiving sterilization clearance.

Do you offer emergency care and ambulance services?

Yes, our Emergency Department and Trauma Response Care are open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. We operate our own fleet of advanced critical care ambulances reachable directly at +1 (800) 555-0198.
