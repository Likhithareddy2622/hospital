Who We Are

Dedicated to Providing World-Class Care

AuraCare Hospital & Research Centre began with a single clinical department and has grown into a superspeciality hospital recognized for healthcare excellence, cutting-edge medical research, and compassionate clinical services.

Our mission is to prioritize human life by making premium medical care accessible and providing personalized therapeutics. We continually invest in state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and recruit the region's top medical specialists.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, comprehensive, and advanced clinical services that improve individual health outcomes.

Our Vision

To remain the regional standard of healthcare excellence through research, innovation, and trust.

Core Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Our Journey

Hospital Growth Milestones

A timeline of our clinical breakthroughs, facility expansion, and patient service improvements.

2012

AuraCare Inception

Started as a specialized outpatient care unit in the city center with 5 consulting doctors and a general health lab.

2016

Cardiology Wing Launch

Constructed a state-of-the-art cardiology ward, introducing advanced diagnostic services and surgical treatments.

2020

Research and ICU Expansion

Expanded local emergency ICU capabilities to 50 beds. Officially established our Medical Clinical Research Wing.

2026

Next-Gen Hospital Building

Moved our main campus to a massive, eco-friendly digital facility with computerized operating suites and robotic tools.
